FAQ

What is Covenant?

Covenant is a decentralized will built on top of the Etherum blockchain. Because of its decentralized nature, Covenant is completely controlled by the blockchain, so payments are secured with the power of millions of computers around the world.

Why should I trust it?

You're free (and encouraged) to check out the source code on Github. If you have any questions or concerns feel free to contact us using the links at the bottom of the page.

How can I use Covenant?

The easiest way to use Covenant is to download the Metamask wallet here, send your Metamask wallet some ether using an exchange of sorts, and filling out the information at the top of the page.

My transaction isn't going through. Why?

Try raising your gas limit for the transaction. Don't worry if it seems high; most of the times, the gas limit isn't even reached.
